Japan

Japan is one of the countries I want to visit most. In fact, if I were given a chance, I want Japan to be the first country that I travel to. Not only that, but I have greatly considered working in Japan, and even settling down there. I am very fond of their culture, and I greatly admire their discipline and environmental consciousness. I am also fascinated with their history, that is why I am keen to visiting historical places when I get the chance to. Here are some of the places I want to visit in Japan:

Osaka Castle

Mount Fuji

Kyoto

Nara

Sapporo
