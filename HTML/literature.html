Sad Songs Are Beautiful

November 29, 2018

Have you ever heard a sad song?
Where the lines speak to your ears
And the message hits your soul.

Have you ever felt a sad song?
Where the tune makes you weak
And your tears continue to fall.

Have you ever loved a sad song?
Where the music, you can't resist
And listening to it, everyday you hope.

Sad songs are beautiful—

An art that can speak
Like a masterpiece with a soul.

Though sad songs are dangerous—

You fall in love with it,
Then it breaks you when it's over.

Music Box

May 10, 2017

The old wooden box
you gave me one winter;
I have kept for life
As my only memento.

As light music sounded
You instantly filled my mind
Full of memories
That I'm too weak to go by.

Tears fell from my eyes
As I quietly asked myself:

Of all the people in this world,
why is it you who must say goodbye?

The City Below

June 15, 2020

You are the city, I am the night sky.
Wretched I am for the murkiness up high,
But it is you who lost my stars.

I have been there to light your way,
So that you will rest from worldly lights.
You are the city, I am the night sky.

You needed me when the sun was out,
But you chose to banish the vivid sight.
It was you who lost my stars.

I withheld the smoke along with its dust,
As my protection slowly faded.
You are the city, I am the night sky.

I see people taking photographs below,
But it is only the moon they can find.
Truly, it was you who lost my stars.

But if I could, I would come back
And give you the light I know you deserve.
Since you are the city, I am the night sky,
But it is you who lost all of my stars.

Oak Tree

December 17, 2018

I have always told you about a love only I can give.
The pain and the suffering, only I can endure.

A faithful heart, unique and true
Who'd be dying to wait under the oak tree for you

I have always promised the things I do for you
Never breaking one, always trying to be true

A smiling face you see that I use to hide my misery
As time goes by under the tree
And as you give love to someone else; but not me.

Remembering

January 17, 2022

When I am left alone
Cold under the deep blue sky
Will time keep its promise
Of keeping memories of me alive?

The thought follows me—
it might stay until my death bed;
walk with peple behind the hearse;
lead me to the afterlife.

I haven't done good—
Hell, I haven't done enough

When will I be satisfied?
Will I be able to go back to the start?

Wide Awake

January 19, 2022

I can't sleep—
a million thoughts
running through my mind.

It never leaves—
it intensifies.
All of it—unkind.

Tick, tock—
time is running out.
The sun will rise again.

I pop one in—
flush it with water.
Alas! My eyes are heavy.

I hope I won't
need another pill
to be able to sleep once again.

Love Dilemma

May 15, 2017

Staying up all night
while staring at photos online
as your only memento of
the person you love.

Love being the reason why
in the morning you look very tired
sleepy eyes with a sleepy mind
you live alone until midnight

The only time you ever get lively
as you get to see the person you love
although the spark is only at your end—
your love interest was numb to its core.

This dilemma you face isn't silly;
the mind is tired,
but your heart keeps pushing on and on
until you completely break down,
into a corner
where you cry all night.

The insatiable heart you own
the rock-solid conscience of the person you long for
never failing to leave a scar you fix alone

And you can never blame anyone
for experiencing this kind of illusion.

Sadyang Nabighani

November 22, 2017

Tuluyan akong nahulog sa'yo
Tila hulog ng langit ang pagkatao
Sadyang nabighani sa isang tulad mo

Bawat pagkakataong makita ka
Ang oras ay biglang huminto
Tuluyan akong nahulog sa'yo.

Sa mga pagkakataong tayo'y magkasama
Di mapigilan ngiti mula sa aking labi
Ako'y nabighani sa isang tulad mo

Di man palaging nagtatagpo ang ating mga mata
Isang sulyap mo lamang, mundo ko'y nabubuo
Tuluyan akong nahulog sa'yo.

Nang iyong malaman ang nararamdaman
Malayang tinanggap ang damdaming ito
Hindi ko napigilang mabighani sa isang tulad mo

Ngunit biglaang nanlamig, hindi na makita
Mala-anghel na katauhang iyong pinamalas
Dahil ba tuluyan akong nahulog sa'yo?
Pasensiya na kung ako'y nabighani sa isang tulad mo.
