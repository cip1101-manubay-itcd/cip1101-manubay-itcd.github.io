Future Plans

What I've planned out so far.

I haven't planned that far into the future yet, because I always try to live in the moment. It saves me from the disappointment of not achieving what I tried to achieve, and it helps me keep in touch of my present dealings. But, for the sake of this article, I will be talking about my plans in general.

Where do you want to read?

Graduation Work Money Settlement Health

If nothing else, you may scroll down to start reading.

Graduate college.

I think everyone who is currently studying in college right now feels the same way as I do. I want to get my bachelor's degree on-time, and then let my future unfold to see if I will pursue post-graduate studies. I think this is the biggest thing that I want to achieve in the closest time-frame available.

Back to Top

Get a job abroad.

I love my country, but I am looking forward to exploring what other countries have to offer. I want to be able to work in a new environment as a challenge for my flexibility and adaptability. I also know that there are bigger opportunities for me outside Philippines, that is why I am set on getting a job overseas as soon as I possibly can.

Back to Top

Earn money.

This may seem very materialistic, but there is a part of me that feels that way. I have been in situations wherein money was a big problem, specifically the scarcity of it. I know how it feels like to not have as much money as I need, and not being able to do anything about it. That is why, my plan is to earn as much money as I can. I want to take this as a repayment to myself and my family, that I am finally able to buy the things I want to buy.

Back to Top

Settle on my own.

While I love my family very much, I also have plans of living separately from them. That does not mean I will cut off my relationship with them, but rather as a lesson on independence and maturity. I want to be able to get to know myself in a deeper level without having to adhere to rules that were set inside a house that I do not own. I love freedom, and I will make sure that I am capable of settling alone before I start doing so.

Back to Top

Keep my health in check.

With the money I earn from my job, I want to make sure that I get regular check-ups, or even regular psychiatric counseling. I know that I am not the healthiest, and my lifestyle is a testament to that. I also know that I am not the most emotionally-stable, and it affects the other aspects of my life. So, as a repayment to myself, for the years of stress, I will make sure that I get the medical attention I need. I still want to live long, and I think that would be the best way for me to see that through.
