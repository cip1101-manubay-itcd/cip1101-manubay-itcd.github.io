Competence: My Skills

When it comes to mastering a skill, time is the magic ingredient.
— Robert Greene

I can do public speaking, because I love to speak.1 My mother has always assisted me in learning how to confidently talk when I was very young.2 Because of this, I have grown to be able to speak without my thoughts bothering me too much.3 I always try to participate whenever I know the topics in a recitation, or join conversations about things I know about.4 Although this helped me make friends, I also have had my share with misunderstandings and quarrels.5 Because of this, I can actively participate in debates about social or political issues.6 Even though I like to participate in these kinds of debates, I am aware of the limits of my knowledge.7 I like memorizing, though.8 Whether it is a literary piece, a speech, an algorithm, or bits of general knowledge.9 It's because I love expanding my knowledge, either general or specific.10 That is why I got to absorb my programming classes in my previous semesters.11 Though if left unused, I know that it will quickly fade away from my subconscience.12

I also sing, because my family is musically-inclined.13 I have done chorale singing, stage play singing, pop singing, and ballad singing.14 However, I do not see myself venturing into this field in a competitive way.15 I also know how to play the guitar, but I am not very good at it.16 I know how to draw, though I am not the best at it.17 Rather, I am fairly confident in my graphic design skills.18 I have been doing some of the publicity matters that COMSSO puts out on their page.19 I also had the opportunity to be able to remaster their logo.20 I have other skills, but I will not discuss them.21 This is because I believe that I should learn more about them to consider them as my skill.22 I am looking forward to learning more skills.23 I know that I am someone who loves trying out new things, so I believe that my skillset will keep expanding.24 And I am certain that the skills I talked about will remain for many years to come.25

13My mother is very fond of music. So, at a very young age, me and my siblings have been exposed to music.
15I see singing as something that I do for recreation, and I would much rather not be stressed by the concept of competitive singing.
19As of writing, I hold the position of AVP for Creative Arts.

Recreation: My Hobbies

You never lose a dream, it just incubates as a hobby.
— Larry Page

I am a fairly simple person, so my interests are quite simple.1 I love watching movies and TV series.2 I honestly prefer watching over reading, because I love moving pictures over blocks of text.3 Do not get me wrong, I also am fine with reading.4 In fact, I have books before, as I have mentioned in my biography.5 However, I can easily empathize with emotion if I see or hear it.6 I mainly watch anime series and movies.7 I love the way that they are produced.8 The art, story, music, and even character designs are important aspects for me.9 I cannot enjoy animes that look good, but lack depth.10 I also cannot thoroughly enjoy animes that are full of depth, but lacking aesthetic.11 I love empathizing with characters, so I can't enjoy animes that lack character development.12 And because I love music, I am not satisfied whenever anime soundtracks don't catch my attention.13

I also love listening to music.14 The genres I listen to are many, and they depend on my mood.15 It can also be in different languages.16 My favorite genres are R&B, Indie, Acoustic, Jazz, Funk, Soul, etc.17 I listen to K-Indie, J-Indie, and Indie OPM.18 I can describe the music I listen to as soft, jazzy, unconventional, and sometimes highly stimulating.19 This is why I pick music depending on my mood.20 I love creating playlists for different kinds of mood or situation.21 I also play games, though I am not into games as much as other people are.22 I like simulation games, like The Sims.23 I have been playing The Sims ever since I was young.24 But due to current circumstances and my fading interest, I no longer play it.25 Right now, I am into League of Legends.26 This is a MOBA game that I play with my friends regularly.27 However, I am not a competitive gamer.28 I would rather play games that I can play at my own pace, because I easily get stressed out if I am met with obligations to play.29 It takes away the fun in playing games for me.30 I am looking forward to other hobbies that might interest me, as long as they're healthy for my mental health.31

9I am interested in the function of the anime industry.
19I like to discover new music that is not mainstream.

Curiosity: My Interests

The mind is not a vessel to be filled, but a fire to be kindled.
— Plutarch

My interests are deeply connected with my current skills and hobbies.1 I am interested in music, specifically in music production and songwriting.2 I have written songs myself, and I find the process fascinating, even if it is sometimes tiring.3 I am also interested in art, specifically in paintings.4 I love how emotions are translated to brush strokes, and how each piece gives you a specific feeling when you look at them.5 That is why I promised myself, that when I go to other countries, I will try to go to art museums to understand their culture through art.6 I am also interested in Japan, specifically in their culture and history.7 I have always wanted to work in Japan, and I am fully aware of the above-average intensity of their work culture.8 I am very fascinated about how they function as a country, and how they manage to instill their values even to younger generations.9 Because of this, I have started learning their language and writing system, though I have a long way to go.10 I am interested in computers, specifically software programming and cybersecurity.11 I am fascinated about how people can protect systems, as well as how people can penetrate these protections.12 I am also quite fond with how software is made.13 I see myself pursuing a job opportunity related to software programming or cybersecurity.14 Another thing that interests me are games, specifically game production and development.15

If software programming and cybersecurity does not work to my favor, I find myself seeking opportunities in the field of game development.16 I am fond of behind the scenes in game development, particularly the way games are designed and developed over time.17 And this interest was intensified by a project we had last semester, wherein we had to individually create games using Java.18 I am proud of the game I made, and I have plans of developing it further.19 I am also interested in graphic design.20 I wish to have proper equipment and software for graphic design in the future.21 I think this will be a part-time job opportunity for me when I graduate.22 And I also love designing myself, so I will probably not get tired of it easily.23 I also want to take proper classes in design to reinforce my skills.24 If given the chance, I would want a certificate or degree in graphic design.25 This is because I am also interested in productions, either in television series or movies.26 I love seeing VFX, CGI, kinetic graphic design, and other kinds of design in moving pictures.27 My interests are dynamic.28 I am sure that I'll have more interests, or lose interests in the following years.29 All I am sure of is that each interest that I have plays an important role in defining who I am.30

16I would love an opportunity in game programming, game art, or game storywriting.
